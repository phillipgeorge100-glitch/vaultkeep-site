Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 April 2026

Your financial data stays on your device. Always.
1. Who We Are

VaultKeep is published by VaultKeep Ltd (Company No. 17033775), registered in England and Wales. Contact: support@vaultkeep.co.uk

2. Our Privacy Promise

VaultKeep is built on a local-first architecture. All your financial data — accounts, holdings, liabilities, assets, snapshots, settings, and themes — is stored on your device and never transmitted to our servers. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no telemetry, and no tracking.

When you use optional paid features, minimal data processing occurs through our server as described below.

4. AI Assistant

If you enable the AI assistant, conversations are sent directly from your device to your chosen AI provider (e.g. OpenAI, Anthropic, Google) using your own API key. VaultKeep Ltd does not see, proxy, intercept, or store any part of these conversations. Your API key is stored locally on your device, optionally encrypted using WebCrypto AES-GCM.

6. International Transfers

Some of the recipients above are based outside the UK. Where personal data is transferred internationally, we rely on:

  • Cloudflare: UK Addendum to Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and Cloudflare's Data Processing Agreement.
  • LemonSqueezy: Data Processing Agreement with SCCs. Payment processing is handled by Stripe.
  • AI providers (BYOK): You have a direct relationship with your chosen provider under their terms.
7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Restriction: Request we restrict processing in certain circumstances.
  • Complaint: Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

To exercise any of these rights, email support@vaultkeep.co.uk. We will respond within one month.

ICO contact: Information Commissioner's Office, Wycliffe House, Water Lane, Wilmslow, Cheshire, SK9 5AF. Tel: 0303 123 1113. Website: ico.org.uk

8. Data Security
  • All financial data is stored locally on your device using localStorage/IndexedDB
  • AI provider API keys are encrypted using WebCrypto PBKDF2 + AES-GCM
  • Licence keys and email addresses are SHA-256 hashed before any server-side storage
  • No raw personal identifiers are ever logged by our server
  • Webhook signatures are verified using timing-safe HMAC-SHA256 comparison
9. What We Don't Collect
  • No personal information beyond what's described above
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No crash reports
  • No cookies (first-party or third-party)
  • No device fingerprints
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No browsing history
10. Children's Privacy

VaultKeep is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly process data from anyone under 18.

11. Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy, we will update this page, the “Last updated” date, and notify you via an in-app banner on the next update.
